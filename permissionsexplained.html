The permissions requested by Prayer Partners from your Google account are:
Permissions Explanations
View your email messages and settings Prayer Partners searches through your mailbox to identify the emails sent by your friends through the app. Friend requests, friend accepts, and updates of items are extracted from these emails to populate your data of the app.
Send email on your behalf Prayer Partners sends friend requests, accepts friend requests, and updates shared items by sending an email using your Gmail account to your friend.
View and manage its own configuration data in your Google Drive Prayer Partners uses the app folder feature to store your data including your friends, prayer item lists, and your configuration. This do not allow the app to access other documents not created by the app itself.
